10 Minute Play

During my time writing a ten minute play I was able to learn a lot about my writing skills. I was able to research and take my knowledge of Spanish and the spanish culture and use that to further develop my writing. I faced some difficulties especially when it came to creating an interesting story and putting that into dialogue however, I overcame that through the writing of this play. Since then, I have finally been able to create interesting dialogue for any given story or situation. I realized that as a writer I am better at developing a character and creating a backstory to personify a character rather than creating a story, which I will continue to work on. My hope is that after reading my work people see how ideas like cultural diffusion are a good way to add flavor to a story.
